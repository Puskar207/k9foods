Our story

It all started with a wag and a wish.

We were frustrated pet parents. Store-bought kibble was full of fillers, imported brands were overpriced, and no one seemed to care about the 35 million strays who eat whatever they find.

So we built K9 from scratch — sourcing chicken, fish, and produce from Indian farms, slow-cooking in small batches, and reinvesting 2% of every bag into stray feeding, shelter vets, and rescue ambulances.

Good to know

Frequently
asked questions.

Everything you need to know before your first bowl. Can't find what you're looking for? Our pet care team is one message away.

How does the subscription work?
Take our pet quiz, pick a cadence (weekly, bi-weekly, or every 4 weeks), and your food arrives on schedule. You save 15% on every order and get free shipping. You can pause, skip, swap recipes, or cancel any time from your dashboard — no emails, no scripts, no guilt trips.
Is K9 suitable for puppies and kittens?
Our adult formulas are designed for pets 12 months and older. Our puppy and kitten recipes (coming soon) pack extra protein, DHA, and calcium for growing tail-waggers. Take the quiz and we'll recommend the right fit.
Where do you ship, and how long does it take?
We ship to every PIN code in India. Metro orders usually arrive in 24–48 hours; other cities within 3–5 business days. Subscribers get priority fulfillment at no extra cost.
What if my pet doesn't like it?
We offer a 100% happiness guarantee on your first order. If they don't dive in, we'll swap the recipe or refund you — no questions asked, no pouches to return. We've got a 96% "clean bowl" rate, but picky pets keep us honest.
How does the "Pawsitive Impact" program work?
2% of every order is pooled monthly and distributed across NGO-run shelters, stray-feeding drives, rescue ambulances, and low-cost animal hospitals. We publish the impact numbers on the 1st of every month.
